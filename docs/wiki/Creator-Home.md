**Thank you** for the interest to spread the word!

My publications are innovative, based on solifd software engeinering principles, and want to educate towards a senior skillset.

CodedConfig is a word play to the "Coded Source Files" of the UiPath Studio. Technically this project is about a custom .NET class with strong typing and and a loader convenience method.

To reduce friction full backward compatibility with REFramework is kept, and the "Getting Started" guide ends in dual use of the `Config` of type Dictionary(Of String, Object) and the `ConFigTree` of type CodedConfig.

This means your content will be additive, and your viewers achieve better code while they can continue to use their legacy code.

The CodedConfig has been battle-tested beyond the documented scope, and can even loosely couple UiPath Studio library projects.

This shall give you, the content creator, a basis for unique, high-quality content, without friction or support requests.
